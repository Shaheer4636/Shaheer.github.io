This paper presents the design, deployment, and evaluation of a multi-tenant Machine Learning Facility-as-a-Service (MLFaaS) built on Kubernetes and Kubeflow. Deployed on bare-metal on-premise servers at the National Centre for Physics (NCP – CoE Aitec), the system supports hosted researchers through LDAP-backed authentication and CPU/memory resource quota controls. A custom web application provides workflow control for ML pipelines within the Aitec research environment.

The work extends research conducted during an internship at NCP under Dr. Muhammad Imran (former CERN scientist) and was continued as a Final Year Project at PIEAS under Dr. Irfan Ul Haq, earning research funding and a perfect GPA.
