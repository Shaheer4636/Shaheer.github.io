This paper investigates how artificial intelligence tools and techniques can augment knowledge sharing and management workflows within educational institutions. It examines both theoretical frameworks and practical deployments, drawing on case studies and empirical analysis to identify how AI-driven systems can improve information dissemination, collaboration, and institutional memory in academic environments.
