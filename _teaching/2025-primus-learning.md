Served as Lead Instructor for the "Python Programming & Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)" bootcamp at Primus Vekuh from February 1, 2025 to May 1, 2025. Delivered **120 hours** of live instruction to a cohort of **25 students**.

## Program Curriculum

The program covered:
- **Python** — fundamentals through OOP and testing with pytest
- **SDLC/DevOps practices** — requirements to maintenance phase
- **Git/GitHub workflows** and CI/CD fundamentals
- **Containerization concepts** and cloud-aware deployment
- **Documentation standards** and collaborative team practices

## Capstone Project

Supervised a capstone project integrating Python, unit tests, and a minimal CI pipeline in GitHub Actions.

## Outcome

Designed weekly objectives, hands-on labs, and graded assessments. Received a formal certification letter from Primus Learning recognizing strong pedagogy, clear communication, and practical command of modern software engineering practices.
