Hello, and welcome!

This is my personal corner of the internet — a space apart from the academic and professional work you might find elsewhere on this site. Here, I write as a person, not just as a researcher or engineer.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Reflections** on learning, growth, and the journey through academia and work
- **Travel & experiences** — places I've been, things I've seen
- **Ideas & observations** that don't fit neatly into a research paper
- **Books, essays, and things I'm thinking about**

## Why a Personal Blog?

There is something worth preserving about the unpolished version of a person — the questions they're sitting with, the small discoveries, the moments that meant something. Research papers and CVs capture what you've accomplished. A blog captures who you're becoming.

I hope you enjoy reading.

— Shaheer
